Generalized Chebyshev polynomials 

 

TMultiply(type,alpha,beta) 

returns the product of generalized Chebyshev Polynomials of the first kind T_alpha * T_beta, alpha, beta in Z^n, in the basis of Chebyshev Polynomials. 

-type: simple Lie type 

-alpha, beta: list 

> TMultiply(G,[1,0],[1,0]);
 

y0,13+y1,03+y2,06+y0,06 (3.1)
 

 

TPolyRecurrence(type,alpha) 

writes the Chebyshev polynomial associated to alpha in Z^n according to a recurrence relation (this is not unique: here we choose to always remove the first positive entry of the argument). 

-type: simple Lie type 

> TPolyRecurrence(G,[2,0])
 

6⁢T1,02−T0,0−2⁢T0,1−2⁢T1,0 (3.2)
 

 

TPoly(type,alpha) 

computes the Chebyshev polynomial associated to alpha in Z^n in the standard monomial basis 

-type: simple Lie type 

-alpha: list 

> TPoly(G,[-2,3])
 

18⁢z12⁢z2+6⁢z12−9⁢z1⁢z2−6⁢z22−2⁢z1−5⁢z2−1 (3.3)
