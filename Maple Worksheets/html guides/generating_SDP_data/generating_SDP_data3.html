Step 1: Choose the hierarchy order "d" 

The minimal order d of the hierarchy is the maximum of the degree of the objective function f and of the degree of the largest constraint. 

> d1:=DegreePoly(Type,n,f);
 

d1≔2 (1.1.1)
 

> d2:=RowDimension(THermiteMatrix(Type,n));
 

d2≔3 (1.1.2)
 

> d_min:=max(d1,d2);
 

d_min≔3 (1.1.3)
 

 

We start at the lowest possible order: 

> d:=d_min;
 

d≔3 (1.1.4)
