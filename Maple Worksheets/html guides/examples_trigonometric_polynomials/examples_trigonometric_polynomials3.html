Remark 2.10. (the matrix defining the image of the generalized cosines) 

The image of the generalized cosines/the T-orbit space of the Weyl group/the feasible region of the optimization problem is the semi-algebraic set, given by the positivity locus of a Hermite matrix polynomial. 

The formula for the matrix is available in the standard monomial basis and in the Chebyshev basis with complex and real variables. 

> Type,n:='A',2; #type, dimension
HermiteMatrix(Type,n); #in the standard monomial basis
RHermiteMatrix(Type,n); #in the standard monomial basis with real variables
THermiteMatrix(Type,n); #in the Chebyshev basis
RTHermiteMatrix(Type,n); #in the Chebyshev basis with real variables
